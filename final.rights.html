Your Rights

If ICE knocks on your door…


DO NOT open the door unless a SIGNED warrant is shown.

IF it IS signed, make sure the name and address are correct.

REMAIN SILENT as anything you say can be used against you.

Say “I plead the fifth and choose to remain silent.”

DO NOT sign anything without an attorney present.

If you fear deportation…


Contact an immigration attorney.

Seek sanctuary at select

CHURCHES, SCHOOLS, and CITIES.

You cannot be deported at these locations.

If you are pulled over…


DO NOT run.

REMAIN SILENT as anything you say can be used against you.

Say “I plead the fifth and I choose to remain silent.”

DO NOT say anything regarding your status without an attorney present.

If you are more than 100 air miles from any U.S. boundary you are not required to show documents.
