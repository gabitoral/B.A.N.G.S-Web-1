What is SB4?


Is a Texas law that was reinforced by local government and law enforcement agencies. It punishes local officers who choose to prioritize their communities’ safety. Police chiefs and Sheriffs across the state we're against it but Governor Abbott signed SB4 on May 7, 2017.

What has changed?


Local officials can still make their own decisions about when and how to ask for federal immigration enforcement assistance, and they don’t face any penalties for declining request for federal immigration agents assistance.

Local officials are free to speak up against laws like SB4 that required cooperation with federal immigration enforcement.
